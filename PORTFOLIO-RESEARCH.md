## Executive Summary

This research provides a complete framework for positioning yourself as a senior Angular developer targeting US/EU clients through freelancing platforms (Upwork, Fiverr) and traditional employment channels (LinkedIn, job boards).

**Key Insight:** Technical skills get you noticed, but enterprise experience and measurable impact get you hired.

**Critical Finding:** Portfolios with published work are hired **9x more often** than those without.
